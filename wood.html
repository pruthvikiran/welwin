Wood Borers

Some type of wood are usually extremely prone to penetration and infestation of powder-post beetles. Afterwards, the entire insect life - cycle occurs within the timber.


Plentiful close exit holes and bore - dust are definite evidence of insect activity. As are extremely high chances of extensive damage to fixtures and fittings. Alongside, by this timbered items can readily become almost useless, nearly always.


Remedial treatment involves sprayed or brushed preservative formulation suitably carried out. Injection filling of the tiny bitten openings as practicably suitable. Both insecticide vaapour fumes and contact action will quickly erode and remove existing infestation.
